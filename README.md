# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard - Power BI

## Problem Statement
Analyze IBM HR data to understand employee attrition patterns and identify 
key factors driving employees to leave the company.

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset (Kaggle) - 1,470 employees 
with 35 attributes including age, department, salary, job satisfaction.

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop, DAX

## DAX Measures Created
- Total Employees = 1,470
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate %
- Average Monthly Income

## Report Pages
- Page 1 (Overview): Attrition by Department, JobRole, Age, Income
- Page 2 (Deep Dive): Job Satisfaction vs Attrition, Years at Company trend

## Key Insights
- Research & Development has highest attrition count
- Lower job satisfaction strongly correlates with higher attrition
- Employees with 0-2 years tenure have highest attrition risk
- Navigation buttons added for seamless page switching

## Features
- 4 DAX measures
- Interactive slicers (Department, Gender, JobRole)
- 2-page report with navigation buttons
- Professional dark theme
